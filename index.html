    Personal Logo

    This is a logo I made that is based on my chinese name, Yik Kiu Fong. The Y and K represent Yik Kiu. The square that surrounds each letter is my last name, Fong. In chinese, Fong also can be defined as a square.

    • Category: Graphic Design

    Personal Poster

    This poster was made based on my personal logo. It is a breakdown of my logo. From Fong, Yik, to Kiu, it is separated at each layer to showcase my name in the traditional chinese way, where chinese characters are written and read from top to bottom. I placed chinese characters of my name within the breakdown of my logo to indicate the counterpart of each letter.

    • Category: Graphic Design

    Wallpaper

    This is a section of a pattern wallpaper that I made to resemble space. I used geometric shapes and thin lines to represent a shining sun surrounded by stars just as colorful as the sun itself.

    Potato

    This gif was made to poke fun at the term 'couch potato'. Instead of a human watching television on a couch and eating potato chips, it is a potato watching television and eating human chunks. Furthermore, a video was made based on this gif in which the potato is watching a cooking show where the cooks are making a dish that traditionally uses potatoes instead.
